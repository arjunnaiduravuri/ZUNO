E-Governance

Key Components of E-Governance

E-Governance refers to the use of digital technology to enhance and streamline the delivery of government services, improve communication between citizens and government, and make administrative processes more efficient. It encompasses a wide range of services and interactions conducted electronically, aimed at increasing transparency, accountability, and accessibility in government operations.
